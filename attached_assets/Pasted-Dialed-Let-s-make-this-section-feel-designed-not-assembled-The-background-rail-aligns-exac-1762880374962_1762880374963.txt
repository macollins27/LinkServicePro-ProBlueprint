## Replace the current “Workflow” section with **Workflow Rail — Aligned Nodes**

### Visual

* Background: very light blue `#F7FAFF`.
* Rail: a dashed blue line that passes through **three fixed nodes** aligned to the card centers at desktop, and adapts to 2-up / 1-up on smaller screens.
* Step badges: large circular blue badges (`48px`) with white numbers, overlapping the card’s top-left corner.
* Card CTAs: subtle pill links (ghost style) with arrow; section uses the only solid button.

### Copy (unchanged, clear language)

* Title: Everything you need in one place
* Subtitle: Plan the day, send estimates, and collect payments without switching tools.
* Cards: Schedule and dispatch • Send estimates and get approval • Invoice and get paid.

### Why this should land

* **Aligned rail**: the line and node dots now correspond to the cards at each breakpoint, so it reads as a real three-step flow.
* **Numbers have presence**: bold circular badges overlap the card and anchor the eye.
* **Less button clutter**: soft pill links on cards + one strong section CTA keeps focus on conversion.
* **Legible and calm**: hard contrast text, white cards, and a purposeful motif.
